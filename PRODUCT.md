# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Researchers, students, and academic staff. They are here to either publish rigorous academic work, manage the editorial pipeline, or find credible research for their own studies.

## Product Purpose
A robust academic publishing platform designed to manage the strict hierarchy of Journals, Volumes, Issues, and Articles, while providing a clean public reading experience.

## Brand Personality
Academic, formal, highly utilitarian, and predictable.

## Anti-references
Vibe-coded AI slop. Overly flashy or trendy SaaS aesthetics. Do not use generic "modern" styles that distract from the academic content.

## Design Principles
1. **Logic over aesthetics:** Focus purely on robust workflows, data integrity, and functional completeness. Do not alter the existing UI style.
2. **Strict Hierarchy:** Enforce the Journal > Volume > Issue > Article structure strictly in the UX.
3. **No surprises:** Keep interactions standard and expected for academic researchers.
4. **Data Density:** Ensure tables and lists show relevant academic metadata clearly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility, ensuring PDFs and abstracts are easily readable and navigatable for all users.
